About Us

The Country’s Best Chicken® takes chicken delivery to the next level. In a small town in Northwest Iowa in 1981, Pizza Ranch created The Country’s Best Chicken® as an item on its popular buffet. Now, our chicken is filling a need for a cravable meal delivered directly to your door. One bite and you’ll understand why we’re called The Country’s Best Chicken®.
